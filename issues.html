Our Objectives

Safer Streets for Pedestrians and Cyclists: Advocate for safer, well-designed sidewalka, bike lanes, and crosswalks to make walking and biking more accessible and secure for everyone Healthier Lifestyles Promote walking and biking as healthy alternatives to driving, helping reduce the risk of lifestyle-related diseases while improving overall fitness and mental well-being Reduce Traffic and Pollution Support policies that reduce car dependency, lowering traffic congestion, noise, and air pollution in local communities. Connect Communities Enhance urban planning to create more walkable and bike-friendly neighborhoods, connecting public spaces, schools, parks, and workplaces.
